Chill Songs

Bills -ENHYPEN

ENHYPEN, a boy group under Pledis, consists of 7 members. They debutted late 2020, and have been popular ever since. Interestinly, the group formed from a survival show called "I-Land" where contestants partcipated in a series of performances to showcase their talents. The judges, viewers, and participants voted for the members to debut. "Bills" is one of their songs in their "Dark Blood" album and is widely loved for its calm vibe and memorable chorus.

Ghosting -TXT

TXT is another boy group under HYBE. They consist of 5 members and debutted in 2019. Their song "Ghosting" is about being cut off from the rest of the world, but the melody is not as melancholy as you would expect from the lyrics. The constrast makes the song a lot more interesting and the instrumentals are also very calming.

Quiet Songs

Try Again -JAEHYUN, d.ear

"Try Again" is about someone who is sure of their emotions, although he is aware his relationship may end soon. The acoustics acoustics and slow beat makes this song perfect for cold winter days or for studying. The artist is a soloist from SM.

WAR-R-R -Colde

ALthough released 9 years ago, "War-r-r" still remains a popular song today. The lyrics are about being lost in a relationship and feeling a little helpless. As Colde sings in a soft tone, the beats in the background bring the listener's attention in, making the song enjoyable to listen to.

Upbeat Songs

Plot Twist -TWS

A fairly new group, TWS debuted in early 2024 with their most recent comeback as "Plot Twist". The catchy beats and cute lyrics make this song all the more nostalgic. The lyrics are about being excited to see your crush and making sure everything is perfect. In fact, this whole album has a similar vibe, so if you like this song, definitely check it out!

Shooting Star -XG

XG is a girl group consisting of 7 members. Although they are based in South Korea, all of them are Japanese, and their songs are in English. Shooting Star went viral around 2023 for it's catchy lyrics and choreography. It's about trusting yourself about your future. Their other song "Left Right" is also listed in the Chill Songs section!
