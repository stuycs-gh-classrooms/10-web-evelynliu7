Plot Twist -TWS

A fairly new group, TWS debuted in early 2024 with their most recent comeback as "Plot Twist". The catchy beats and cute lyrics make this song all the more nostalgic. In fact, this whole album has a similar vibe, so if you like this one, definitely check it out!
