MAGO by GFRIEND

The message of the song "MAGO" is to embrance your own desires and express them without fear. The girls' refreshing and soft voices combine really well with track's retro instrumentals to create an upbeat song. It's perfecting for dancing and vibing to, and the bright sparkles in the MV add a nice touch.

Feel Special by TWICE

The concept of this song is very elegant as the group sings about how someone makes them feel special just by being there for them. It was more specifically about how the rest of TWICE helped their member Sana when she was going through a rough time.

Spicy by aespa

"Spicy" is a very assertive song. It emphasizes confidence, individuality, and energy, where the lyrics are about how the members are unattainable and confident in their unique qualities. This is emphasized through the energetic beats and dynamic bass sounds in the track.
